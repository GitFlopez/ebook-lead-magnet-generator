# Marketing Brief: Ebook & Lead Magnet Generator

## One-Line Description
Generate complete lead magnets in hours — ebook outline, chapter content, opt-in page copy, and 8-email campaign — without a ghostwriter.

## Marketplace Listing Copy

**Title:** Ebook & Lead Magnet Generator
**Tagline:** Turn your expertise into a list-building machine — outline to launch emails in one day.

### Description (ClawHub marketplace)

Every marketer knows the formula: give away something valuable → build your email list → convert subscribers to customers. The problem? Creating a quality lead magnet takes 20-40 hours of writing work most people don't have.

This skill does the heavy lifting.

**4 prompts. Full lead magnet lifecycle:**

**Prompt 1 — Ebook Outline Generator**
Complete content architecture in 10 minutes: 5 title options, 8-12 chapter outlines with summaries, before/after transformation framework, table of contents, and appendix suggestions.

**Prompt 2 — Chapter Content Writer**
One complete chapter per run — hook, 3-5 sections with examples, action exercise, summary, and transition teaser to the next chapter. Maintains consistent voice across every chapter.

**Prompt 3 — Landing Page & Opt-In Copy**
Full opt-in page copy: 5 headline variants, benefit bullets, form copy, trust signals, thank-you page, and a content upgrade version for embedding in blog posts.

**Prompt 4 — Launch Email Sequence**
5 promotional emails (teaser → launch → value-add → objection handler → final push) + 3 nurture emails for new subscribers (delivery → value → bridge to next offer).

**Built for:**
- B2B marketers building gated content
- Coaches building authority lead magnets
- SaaS founders driving trial signups
- Course creators using ebooks as top-of-funnel
- Agencies producing client lead magnets

**Replaces:** $500-$3,000 ghostwriter, weeks of work, $49/month AI subscriptions

One-time download. All 4 prompts. Full example included.
