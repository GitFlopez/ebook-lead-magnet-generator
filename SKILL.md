## What This Skill Does

Generates complete ebook and lead magnet content — from chapter outline to landing page to promotional emails. Four specialized prompts cover the full lead magnet lifecycle: content architecture, chapter writing, opt-in page copy, and launch email sequence.

## The 4 Prompts

### Prompt 1: Ebook Outline Generator
Produces a complete ebook architecture — title, subtitle, reader promise, 8-12 chapter titles with summaries, key takeaways per chapter, and a transformation framework (before/after state). Includes a table of contents draft and recommended word count per section.

**Input:** Topic, target reader, desired outcome, format (how-to / framework / checklist / case study collection)

### Prompt 2: Chapter Content Generator
Writes one full chapter — intro hook, 3-5 main sections with subheadings, examples/case studies, actionable exercises, and a chapter summary with transition teaser to the next chapter. Maintains consistent voice and reading level throughout.

**Input:** Chapter title, key points to cover, target reader, tone, word count target

### Prompt 3: Landing Page & Opt-In Copy Generator
Writes the complete lead magnet landing page — headline (5 options), subheadline, benefit bullets (what they'll learn), social proof block, opt-in form copy, and thank-you page text. Includes a content upgrade variant for embedding within blog posts.

**Input:** Ebook title, target reader, top 3 benefits, existing social proof (optional), CTA action (download / access / get instant access)

### Prompt 4: Launch Email Sequence Generator
Writes a 5-email promotional sequence to drive downloads — teaser email, launch day email, value-add follow-up (chapter excerpt), objection handler, and final reminder with urgency. Plus a 3-email nurture sequence for new subscribers after they download.

**Input:** Ebook topic, audience pain point, what subscribers get after download, brand voice

## Technical Notes
